Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to CottageWare ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and password when you register for an account.
  • Profile Information: Profile pictures, account tier, and any additional information you choose to provide in your user profile.
  • Communication Data: Messages posted in forums, threads, and private forums.
  • Payment Information: If applicable, for product purchases (processed through secure third-party payment processors).

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our website:

  • IP address and browser information
  • Pages you visit and features you use
  • Time spent on the website and interaction patterns
  • WebSocket connection data for real-time features

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services, including forums and messaging features
  • To manage your account and provide you with customer support
  • To process transactions and manage product orders
  • To personalize your experience and deliver content relevant to your interests
  • To improve our website, products, and services
  • To communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security breaches
  • To display online users and their activity status in forums

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Other Users: Your username, profile information, and content you post in public areas are visible to other users.
  • For Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.
  • With Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as a business asset.

5. WebSocket Connections and Real-Time Features

Our platform uses WebSocket technology to provide real-time features such as chat, forum updates, and online user status. When using these features:

  • We collect connection data to maintain the service
  • Your online status may be visible to other users
  • Messages sent through WebSockets are stored in our database
  • We implement reconnection mechanisms to ensure service reliability

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to certain exceptions)
  • Object to processing of your information
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and track information about your browsing activities. You can set your browser to refuse all or some cookies, but this may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@cottageware.com
